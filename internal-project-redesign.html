INTERNAL PROJECT REDESIGN (Click Image)

B

When our client reviewed an existing screen from a previously designed platform with a new stakeholder, it prompted a round of fresh ideas and a request for a comprehensive redesign. Given my familiarity with both the internal project history and the legacy platform, I was brought in to lead the effort under a tight deadline.

I opened the process by gathering feedback from senior team members, using their insights alongside the client's new direction to identify opportunities for meaningful improvement. Alongside the visual redesign, I also overhauled the component and token system within the working file — restructuring it for greater consistency and efficiency to make future design handoffs significantly smoother.

For design direction, I drew inspiration from a range of successful fintech platforms, selectively borrowing patterns that fit our specific use case and synthesizing them into a cohesive skeleton and component library tailored to the product. The outcome was a design that felt decisively more modern while consolidating previously fragmented page elements — giving users immediate access to more information at a glance and reducing the need to navigate deeper to find what they need.

The project balanced speed with craft: delivering a polished, forward-looking redesign within a constrained timeline while laying stronger groundwork for the platform's long-term scalability.
